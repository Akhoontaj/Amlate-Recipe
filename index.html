Amlate Recipe

Now we are going to make Egg Amlate Recipe

Ingredients

  • 02 x Eggs
  • 1/2 onion
  • o1 x tomato
  • 02 x tespoon oil
  • Salt on need basis
  • Amlate masala

Method

  1. First of all cut the onion & tomato in small pieces
  2. keep the frypan on the burner & put the oil in it
  3. put the onion & tomato in the hot oil and mix it with the spoon for 2,3 mints
  4. Break the eggs and put it into the frypan
  5. the past of onion & tomato may mix with the eggs for 2,3 minuts
  6. Amlate Recipe is ready for eating
